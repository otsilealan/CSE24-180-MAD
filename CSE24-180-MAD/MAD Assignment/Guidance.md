The introduction should motivate the need for your application and tools for
your chosen application area and why it is so important, the tools and
software used. The Explain the purpose of your Application section should
discuss the challenges and business opportunities facing your chosen area.
What benefits you want for your organization out of the whole business
strategy process. Give details of any business rules or strategies. The Describe
your design, database schema, screenshots section should discuss the design
process, data used, database schema etc.… Discuss the issues/challenges your data poses, either complexity or sheer
volume. The Software Products section should focus on the actual software
that incorporates the data environment and data management tools, along
with development tools that would be suitable for your application. Discuss:
platform hardware/software requirements, how data can be displayed, links
to databases.
Use charts, diagrams and tables, where they are appropriate. Probably use 4
6 diagrams and discuss them. Use tables if appropriate. You also need to use
about 5-6 references (URL’s) to show source of software tools etc. Use a
mixture of PowerPoint slides (bullet points, diagrams etc.), and live actions of
software you have used etc.  Obviously, this requires your “voice over”
describing the slides and scenes. Use the mouse pointer as an aid to help the
audience look at whatever you are describing. Plan and practice your
screencast before submission.
